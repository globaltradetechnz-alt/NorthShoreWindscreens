Which Insurers We Deal With

We handle claims with every major New Zealand insurer, including:

  • AA Insurance — comprehensive and third-party policies with windscreen cover
  • State Insurance — windscreen claims managed directly with State
  • AMI Insurance — full claim handling and direct invoicing
  • Vero Insurance — commercial and private vehicle windscreen claims
  • FMG — rural and commercial vehicle glass claims
  • Tower Insurance — comprehensive cover windscreen replacements

How the Claim Process Works

1

Check Your Policy

Confirm your policy includes windscreen cover. Most comprehensive policies do. Note your excess and policy number.

2

Call Us

Phone 09 444-5556 with your vehicle details and insurer. We can start the claim and book your mobile appointment on the North Shore.

3

We Manage the Paperwork

We deal with your insurer, source the correct glass and invoice them directly. You usually just pay your excess.

4

Mobile Fitting

We come to your home or workplace anywhere on the North Shore, replace the glass to manufacturer spec and advise the safe drive-away time.

Do You Have to Pay an Excess?

Most comprehensive policies cover windscreen replacement with a reduced or zero excess. Third-party policies typically do not cover windscreen damage. We can check this with your insurer when you call.

Not Insured?

No problem — we also handle private jobs. We'll give you a firm, fair price up front and replace your glass with the same quality and care. Call 09 444-5556 for a price.

Tip: If your policy has windscreen cover, using it usually does not affect your no-claims bonus. Check with your insurer or ask us when you call.
