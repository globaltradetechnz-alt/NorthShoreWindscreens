Full windscreen, side glass and back glass replacement across the North Shore. Mobile, same-day service and insurance claims handled.

Windscreen Replacement

Your windscreen is a structural part of your vehicle. We replace cracked and smashed windscreens with OEM-quality laminated glass, bonded to manufacturer specification. Sensor and camera transfer included where fitted, and we advise on ADAS recalibration for newer vehicles.

Side Glass

Door, side and quarter glass replaced and sealed weather-tight. We clean out broken glass and restore your vehicle's security. All makes and models covered across the North Shore.

Back & Rear Glass

Rear and back glass replacement including heated screens and wiper-fitted units. Bonded or rubber-set to manufacturer spec, with full clean-up and disposal of the old glass.

Insurance Claims

Most of our work is insurance-based. We deal with all major NZ insurers — AA, State, AMI, Vero, FMG, Tower — manage the paperwork and invoice your insurer directly. Usually you only pay your excess, if any.

Mobile Fitting

We come to your home, workplace or job site anywhere on the North Shore. No need to drive on damaged glass or lose a working day. Most jobs completed in around two hours with safe drive-away times clearly explained.

Small chip? We only do full replacements. For repairable chips, call the chip-repair specialists on 0800 202 888.
