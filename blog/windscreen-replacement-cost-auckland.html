Windscreen Replacement Cost in Auckland

What North Shore drivers typically pay, what affects the price, and how to get a firm price before work begins.

If you're staring at a cracked windscreen and wondering what it will cost to replace, you're not alone. This guide covers typical prices for Auckland and North Shore drivers, what pushes the price up or down, and how to get a firm price before any work starts.

Typical Cost: $250 to $500

Across Auckland, most standard passenger car windscreen replacements fall between $250 and $500. That range covers the glass itself, the urethane bonding kit, mouldings and the labour to fit it safely.

  • Common Japanese and Korean vehicles (Toyota, Honda, Mazda, Hyundai, Kia) — usually at the lower end, $250–$350
  • European vehicles (BMW, Mercedes, VW, Audi) — often $350–$500+ due to OEM glass and sensor calibration
  • Luxury or rare models — can exceed $500 if specialist glass is required

What Affects the Price?

Several factors push the cost up or down:

  • Glass type — OEM (original equipment manufacturer) glass costs more than aftermarket, but matches your vehicle exactly
  • Sensors and cameras — vehicles with rain sensors, lane-keep cameras or ADAS need recalibration, adding cost
  • Heated windscreens — heated or top-tinted glass is more expensive than standard
  • Mouldings and clips — some vehicles need new mouldings every time; others reuse the old ones
  • Mobile service — mobile fitting is usually included in the price on the North Shore

Insurance vs Private Pay

If you have comprehensive insurance with windscreen cover, you usually only pay your excess — often $0 to $100. We invoice your insurer directly. If you're paying privately, we give you a firm price upfront before we order any glass.

How to Get a Firm Price

The fastest way to get an exact price is to call us on 09 444-5556 with your:

  • Vehicle make, model and year
  • Registration number
  • Description of the damage
  • Insurance company (if claiming)

We can usually give you a firm price over the phone, or you can use our Help Form and we'll call you back.

Your actual price may vary based on your specific vehicle and glass requirements. The figures above are a guide for typical Auckland and North Shore vehicles.
