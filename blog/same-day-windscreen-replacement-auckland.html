Same-Day Windscreen Replacement in Auckland

When same-day replacement is possible, how to book it, and what to expect across the North Shore.

A cracked windscreen doesn't have to mean days off the road. For many common vehicles on the North Shore, we can source and fit your new glass the same day you call. Here's how it works and when it's possible.

When Is Same-Day Possible?

Same-day replacement is most likely when:

  • Your vehicle is a common make and model (Toyota, Honda, Mazda, Hyundai, Ford, Holden, etc.)
  • The glass is available from our local supplier network
  • You call early in the day — before 12pm gives us the best chance
  • You are on the North Shore or in central Auckland

For rare or specialist vehicles, or glass that needs to be ordered from overseas, same-day may not be possible. In those cases we source the glass as fast as we can and book you in the moment it arrives.

How to Book Same-Day Service

The fastest way to secure a same-day slot is to call us directly on 09 444-5556. Have ready:

  • Vehicle make, model and year
  • Registration number
  • Your suburb on the North Shore
  • Whether you're claiming on insurance or paying privately

We check glass availability immediately, confirm a time window and come to your home or workplace.

What Happens During the Fitting?

Most windscreen replacements take around two hours. We:

  • Remove the damaged glass and clean the frame
  • Apply manufacturer-approved urethane bonding
  • Fit the new glass and reinstall mouldings
  • Transfer sensors and cameras where fitted
  • Advise the safe drive-away time — usually 1–2 hours in dry weather

Insurance Claims and Same-Day

Even if you're claiming on insurance, same-day is often possible. We can start the claim over the phone, fit the glass, and invoice your insurer afterwards. We deal with AA, State, AMI, Vero, FMG, Tower and all other major NZ insurers.

North Shore Suburbs We Cover Same-Day

We offer same-day mobile service across all North Shore suburbs, including Takapuna, Devonport, Milford, Belmont, Bayswater, Northcote, Birkenhead, Beach Haven, Birkdale, Glenfield, Hillcrest, Forrest Hill, Albany, Browns Bay, Mairangi Bay, Murrays Bay, Campbells Bay, Castor Bay, Torbay, Long Bay, Greenhithe, Dairy Flat, Gulf Harbour, Hibiscus Coast, Manly, North Harbour, Orewa, Red Beach, Silverdale and more.

Tip: Calling before midday gives us the best chance of fitting you in the same day, especially during busy periods.
